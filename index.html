  • JSON and Markdown export
  • No cloud by default

FAQ

Questions before you begin.

Does it work without internet?

The journal works without AI. Internet is needed for AI interpretation and image generation.

Where are my dreams stored?

Dreams are stored locally on your device by default using SwiftData.

Can I use it without AI?

Yes. You can save dreams without interpretation.

Is Somery a medical tool?

No. Somery is for reflection and journaling, not diagnosis or medical advice.

Which languages are supported?

English, Español, Français, Русский, Українська.

How do AI credits work?

Credits are used for AI-powered actions such as interpretation and visualization.
