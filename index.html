About

A small studio with a product-first mindset.

Lunora is currently focused on building mobile-first digital products with simple interfaces, thoughtful AI features, and strong respect for user privacy.

The studio explores tools that help people better understand their thoughts, routines, emotions, and creative ideas without turning the experience into noise.

Simple, maintainable products are better than overloaded feature sets.
